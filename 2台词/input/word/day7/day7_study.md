## attire

**meaning:** 服装，衣着 / clothing, especially of a particular or formal kind

**example dialogues:**

> **In the Name of God (2007)**
> A: Attire? Islamic attire?
> （服装？伊斯兰服装？）
> B: Yes, we're discussing westernized youngsters and Islamic attire.
> （是的，我们在讨论西化的年轻人和伊斯兰服装。）

> **The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City (2021)**
> A: Look at this. Mine is "Attire: Italian Street Fashion."
> （看看这个。我的是“着装要求：意大利街头时尚。”）
> B: - [scoffs] With different attire? - Different attire.
> （- [嘲笑] 要穿不同的服装？- 不同的服装。）
