# day9 - study notes

## undertone

**meaning:** 潜在含义；弦外之音；低沉的声调 (an underlying quality, feeling, or meaning; a subdued or low tone of voice)

**example dialogues:**

> **The Interest of Love (2022)**
> A: -What's my undertone?
> (我有什么弦外之音？)
> B: -You know what it is.
> (你自己知道的。)

> **The West Wing (2005)**
> "Same extortionate undertone?"
> ("还是那种敲诈勒索的弦外之音吗？")

> **I Love Lucy (1954)**
> (undertone): Posture.
> (低声说：注意姿态。)
