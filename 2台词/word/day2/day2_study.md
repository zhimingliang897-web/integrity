# day2 - study notes

## mechanism

**meaning:** 机制；机械装置 (a system of parts working together, or a device with moving parts)

**example dialogues:**

> **The Highest of Stakes (2023)**
> "Finance in general is a mechanism by which the impatient borrow from the patient."
> (总的来说，金融是一种机制，没耐心的人通过它向有耐心的人借钱。)

> **Ölümlü Dünya (2018)**
> A: This is a mechanism.
> (这是一个机械装置。)
> B: Mechanism.
> (机械装置。)

## espouse

**meaning:** 拥护；信奉；支持 (to adopt or support a cause, belief, or way of life)

**example dialogues:**

> **Numb3rs (2009)**
> A: We don't espouse violence.
> (我们不提倡暴力。)
> B: I know, but sometimes force is the only language they understand.
> (我知道，但有时候武力是他们唯一能听懂的语言。)

> **Billions (2023)**
> A: Do you agree with their aggressive tactics?
> (你赞同他们激进的策略吗？)
> B: I don't espouse it, but I understand why they feel it's necessary.
> (我不支持这种做法，但我理解他们为何觉得有必要。)
