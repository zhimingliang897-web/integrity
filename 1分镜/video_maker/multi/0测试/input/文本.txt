English Title — Test Day Jitters — 考试日小紧张  

F1: Hey, are you ready for the math test in ten minutes? | 嘿，十分钟后数学考试，你准备好了吗？  
M1: Barely—I just reread Chapter 3 and panicked twice. | 差点没准备好——我刚重读了第三章，慌了两次。  
---  
F1: Same! I forgot how to solve quadratic equations this morning. | 我也是！今早连二次方程怎么解都忘了。  
M1: Wait—did Ms. Lee say calculators are allowed? | 等等——李老师说计算器能用吗？  
---  
F1: Yes! She emailed it yesterday. Look—here’s the screenshot. | 对！她昨天发邮件通知了。看——这是截图。  
M1: Oh thank goodness. My brain just rebooted. | 啊太好了，我的脑子刚刚重启成功。  
---  
F1: Quick tip: Skip hard questions first, then circle back. | 小贴士：先跳过难题，最后再回头做。  
M1: Genius. And breathe—inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 6. | 太棒了。还有，深呼吸——吸气4秒，屏住4秒，呼气6秒。  
---  
F1: Bell’s ringing! Let’s go—no more panic, just focus. | 铃响了！走吧——别再慌，只管专注。  
M1: Right. Blue pen, ID, and calm confidence. Let’s do this. | 没错。蓝色签字笔、学生证，还有冷静的自信。我们上！  

===  
breathe — 深呼吸（缓解紧张） — “Just breathe—you’ve studied for this.”  
skip hard questions — 先跳过难题 — “I’ll skip hard questions and tackle them after the easy ones.”  
circle back — 回头再做 — “Don’t get stuck—circle back when time allows.”  
reboot (one’s brain) — （口语）让大脑重启/恢复清醒 — “After coffee, my brain finally rebooted.”  
panicked — 慌了（过去式） — “I panicked when I saw the word ‘derivative’.”  
calm confidence — 冷静的自信 — “She walked in with calm confidence—not a trace of stress.”  
allowed — 允许的 — “Is food allowed in the exam hall?”  
focus — 专注（动词/名词） — “Close your eyes and focus on your breath for 10 seconds.”
