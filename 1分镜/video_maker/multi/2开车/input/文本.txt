What’s for Dinner? — 晚饭吃啥？  
M1: Hey! Didn’t expect to see you here—pulling over real quick! | 嘿！没想到在这儿碰到你——我马上靠边停一下！  
F1: Oh wow, perfect timing—I was just scrolling food apps and hitting a wall. | 哇，太巧了！我正刷外卖App，完全想不出吃啥！  
M1: Same. My brain’s officially on lunch break. | 我也是。脑子已经自动进入午休模式了。  
---  
M1: Ugh, every place I check says “30–45 min delivery.” Who has that kind of patience? | 天啊，我看的每家都说“30–45分钟送达”，谁有这耐心啊？  
F1: Tell me about it. I even clicked “vegetarian” just to narrow it down—and got *noodles*. Again. | 别提了！我连“素食”都点了，就想缩小范围——结果又跳出面条！  
M1: Haha, same. My “healthy” filter once gave me a burrito bowl with extra cheese. | 哈哈，我也一样！有次我选“健康餐”，结果给我推了个加双倍芝士的卷饼碗。  
---  
F1: Wait—did you just say “burrito bowl”? | 等等——你刚说“卷饼碗”？  
M1: Yeah? Why? | 对啊？咋了？  
F1: …You’re parked outside *Taco Loco*. The one with the giant neon sombrero. | …你正停在Taco Loco门口呢，就是那个顶上有个巨型霓虹草帽的店。  
---  
M1: Oh. *Oh.* | 哦……哦！！  
F1: So… walk in? Or keep pretending we’re doing deep culinary research from the car? | 所以……直接下车进去？还是继续假装我们在车里做严肃美食调研？  
M1: Nah—I’ll grab us two “no-cheese” bowls. Promise. | 算了，我进去给我们各点一碗不加芝士的。保证。  
===  
What’s for dinner? — 晚饭吃啥？ — What’s for dinner? is the go-to opener when deciding meals casually (not formal).  
I’m hitting a wall — 卡壳了/想不出主意了 — I’ve been brainstorming names for 20 minutes—I’m totally hitting a wall.  
My brain’s on [X] break — 我脑子正在[某状态]休息中（幽默自嘲） — My brain’s on coffee break until at least 10 a.m.  
Who has that kind of patience? — 谁能忍得了这个？（夸张吐槽） — The line’s 20 people deep—who has that kind of patience?  
Tell me about it — 我太懂了！（强烈共情） — “This Wi-Fi is so slow.” “Tell me about it—I rebooted three times.”  
We’re doing [absurd thing] research — 我们在假装认真研究[荒唐事]（轻幽默自嘲） — “Should we use avocado or hummus?” “Are we doing gourmet sandwich research now?”
