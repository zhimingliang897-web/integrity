English Title — Let’s Go Swimming! — 一起去游泳吧！

M1: Hey, ready to hit the pool? I brought my new goggles! | 嘿，准备去游泳池了吗？我带了新泳镜！  
M2: Yeah! Did you check if the indoor pool is open today? | 是啊！你查过室内泳池今天开放吗？  
---  
F1: It is! I just called — they’re open till 8 p.m. | 开着呢！我刚打电话确认过——营业到晚上8点。  
F2: Perfect. I packed towels and sunscreen — it’s sunny even indoors! | 太好了。我带了毛巾和防晒霜——连室内都阳光明媚呢！  
---  
M1: Wait — where’s the locker room? I need to change. | 等等——更衣室在哪儿？我得换衣服。  
M2: Down the hall, left after the water fountain. | 走廊尽头，喷水池左转。  
---  
F1: Oh no — my flip-flops broke! Can I borrow one pair? | 啊糟糕——我的人字拖断了！能借我一双吗？  
F2: Sure! Here — try these blue ones. They’re comfy. | 当然！给你——试试这双蓝色的，很舒服。  
---  
M1: Alright, let’s warm up first — 5 minutes of easy laps? | 好了，先热身吧——游5分钟轻松圈？  
M2: Deal! But no racing — last time you splashed me *twice*. | 成交！但不准比赛——上次你溅了我两次水！  
---  
F1: Ready? On your mark… go! | 准备好了吗？各就各位……开始！  
F2: *(laughing, swimming)* Too fast — I’m still doing breaststroke! | （笑着游泳）太快了——我还在蛙泳呢！

===
core expressions  
1. hit the pool — 去游泳（非正式） — Let’s hit the pool before lunch!  
2. check if… is open — 查看……是否开放 — I’ll check if the gym is open on Sunday.  
3. pack towels and sunscreen — 带好毛巾和防晒霜 — Always pack towels and sunscreen — pools get chilly!  
4. where’s the locker room? — 更衣室在哪儿？ — Excuse me, where’s the locker room?  
5. my flip-flops broke — 我的人字拖断了 — My flip-flops broke on the way here — so embarrassing!  
6. on your mark… go! — 各就各位……开始！（起跑口令） — We shouted “on your mark… go!” and raced to the diving board.  
7. no racing — 别比赛（口语中表“不比速度”） — No racing — just enjoy the water!  
8. I’m still doing [stroke] — 我还在做……泳姿 — I’m still doing breaststroke — backstroke feels weird!
